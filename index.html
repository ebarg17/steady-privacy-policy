Privacy Policy for Steady (Beta)

Last updated: April 14, 2025

Welcome to Steady, a supportive space for reflection and emotional well-being. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information during our TestFlight beta period.

🔐 What We Collect

Data Collected? Details
Email address ✅ If you choose to create an account Used to sign in and personalize your experience.
First name & identity ✅ If you provide it Used only to personalize how Steady addresses you.
Journal entries ✅ If signed in Stored securely so you can revisit or edit past reflections.
Push notification token ✅ If you opt in Used only to send occasional supportive check-ins.
App usage / analytics ❌ No tracking or analytics
Sensitive health data ❌ Not collected

❌ What We Don’t Do

  • No third-party trackers
  • No ads
  • No sharing or selling your data
  • No access to contacts, camera, or location

📬 Push Notifications

If you opt in, Steady may send you occasional, supportive reminders or journaling prompts. You can opt out anytime in your device settings. We promise not to spam you.

🗄️ Data Storage

All journal entries and profile info are stored securely using Supabase. Only you can access your entries.

🧼 How to Delete Your Data

Email hello@hellosteady.app to delete your account and related data.

✉️ Questions?

This is a beta app, and your privacy matters deeply to us. Contact us at hello@hellosteady.app.
